Privacy Policy

CarHelper · Last updated: 17 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CarHelper (“the App”) handles your information. Contact: nutritionhelpthebest@gmail.com.

1. Summary

  • Your garage data (cars, expenses, maintenance, trips) stays on your device.
  • Internet is used only for optional features such as the AI assistant and keeping the App up to date.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Subscriptions are handled by Apple.

2. Data on your device

Depending on how you use the App, it may store on your device:

  • Vehicle details
  • Expenses and maintenance records
  • Trip and fuel history
  • AI chat history (if you use the assistant)
  • App settings (language, units, currency)

This data is not uploaded to a CarHelper account. Deleting the App removes it from the device (except iOS backups you control).

3. When we process data online

If you use the AI assistant, your messages and related car information from the App (for example make, model, year, or recent expenses) may be sent securely over the internet so the assistant can reply. Please do not enter sensitive personal information you do not want processed.

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple under Apple’s privacy terms.

4. Why we process data

  • To provide App features
  • To power the optional AI assistant
  • To manage subscriptions through Apple

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is shared only as needed to run the App (for example AI replies and Apple payments).

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. Do not use it if you are under 13.

7. Your choices

  • You can use garage features without using AI.
  • You can delete chat history or App data on the device.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions: nutritionhelpthebest@gmail.com
