Privacy Policy

CarHelper · Last updated: 17 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CarHelper (“the App”) handles information. The App is developed by an independent developer. Contact: nutritionhelpthebest@gmail.com.

1. Summary

  • Your garage data (cars, expenses, maintenance, trips) stays on your device.
  • We use network services only for app configuration and the optional AI assistant.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Subscriptions are billed by Apple, not by us directly.

2. Data stored on your device

Depending on how you use the App, it may store locally:

  • Vehicle details and odometer
  • Expenses and maintenance records (including optional attachments you add)
  • Trip and fuel calculation history
  • AI chat history for the Garage Advisor
  • Settings (language, units, currency)
  • An anonymous installation identifier used only for on-device experiments (e.g. paywall A/B)

This data is not uploaded to a CarHelper cloud account. Deleting the App or clearing its data removes it from the device (subject to iOS backups you control).

3. Data processed online

When features that need the internet are used, limited data may be sent to:

  • Supabase (our backend): to load remote configuration (feature flags, AI limits) and to run an Edge Function that proxies AI requests.
  • OpenRouter (via our server only): to generate AI replies. The OpenRouter API key is stored on the server, not inside the App.
  • Apple: for In-App Purchases / subscriptions (StoreKit). Apple processes payment under Apple’s privacy terms.

AI requests may include your chat messages and car-related context built from data on the device (for example make/model/year and recent expenses or maintenance summaries). Do not enter sensitive personal information you do not want processed by these providers.

4. Purpose of processing

  • Provide and improve App features
  • Power the AI assistant and enforce free-tier limits
  • Apply remote configuration without an App Store update
  • Manage CarHelper Pro subscriptions through Apple

5. Sharing

We share data with service providers only as needed to operate the App (Supabase, OpenRouter, Apple). We do not sell your data to third parties for advertising.

6. Security

Network traffic uses HTTPS. The App uses a public Supabase key designed for clients; access to configuration is restricted to read-only policies. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. Do not use it if you are under 13.

8. Your choices

  • You can use core garage features without using AI.
  • You can delete chat history or App data on the device.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy: nutritionhelpthebest@gmail.com
